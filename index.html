Introduction About
School for Half-Blood

School Half-Blood is a place of training and protection for demigod children. These children are the descendants of ordinary humans who have one god or goddess as a parent. Because of the divine blood that flows within them, demigods are often the targets of monsters and threats to the mystical world. The School Half-Blood provides safe haven from these dangers, as it is surrounded by a magical barrier that only certain creatures can penetrate. In addition to daily training, the demigods at the School Half-Blood are often sent on dangerous missions to complete tasks ordered by the gods or to fight major threats that threaten the world. The School For Half Blood becomes a kind of second home for them, a place where they can learn more about their true selves, make friends with fellow demigods, and face their destiny as descendants of gods.
