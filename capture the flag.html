Capture The Flag
set up & Rules

set up
1. Teams: Two teams, often representing different cabins (or gods).
2. Field: A designated area is marked out, typically with boundaries and specific zones (like a river or woods).
3. Flags: Each team has a flag, usually a colored cloth or banner, placed in their territory.

Rules
1. Starting Point: Teams start at their respective bases, usually a set distance apart.
2. Capture: Players must enter the opponent's territory to grab their flag.
3. agging: If a player is tagged by an opponent while in their territory, they may be "out" (or frozen) and need to return to their base to re-enter the game.
4. Returning the Flag: Once a player captures the opposing flag, they must make it back to their own base without being tagged.
5. Winning: The game ends when one team successfully captures the other teamâ€™s flag and brings it back to their base.
